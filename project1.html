500KW on Grid SPV plant NIT Rourkela ,Orissa

GENERATION PER DAY : 20,000 UNITS PER DAY

SAVING PER YEAR : Rs 5,110,000/-

TECHNOLOGIES USED : Tier 1 Solar Panels , Grid Tie Solar Inverter , AC & DC CABLES

  • Design Type :The solar panel structure assembly is drilled along the same time as the current sheet is bored with the column. Rails support the weight of solar panels and are secured with the chemical anchoring. It prevents water leakage. The Solar Panels are then bolted and attached to the Rails at the joints. Rails are made of stainless steel or HQ MS coated steel.
  • We only integrate and recommend Tier I solar panels that use 'A' grade cells with a cell efficiency of over 20%. The manufacturers we recommend are global enterprises with gigawatts of installed capacity around the world, as well as the necessary certifications and a 30-year warranty. We chose solar panels based on the manufacturer's credibility.
  • The Solar Panel Structure Components are drilled simultaneously with the current sheet's column boring. Rails support the weight of Solar Panels and are secured with chemical anchoring to prevent any sort of water leakage.
  • We recommend integrating the best German technology inverters because they are the heart of the solar plant. PV systems that are grid connected are designed to run in parallel with and be connected to the electric utility grid. Inverters of excellent standard can last up to 25 years
  • We integrate all of the small and large equipment needed for the Solar Power Plant's operation. The Panel and Inverter manufacturers follow the installation recommendations and manuals for every equipment, including cabling and installation methodology.
