20KW on Grid SPV plant Neelkanth, Haldwani

GENERATION PER DAY : 80 UNITS PER DAY

SAVING PER YEAR : Rs 204 ,400/-

TECHNOLOGIES USED : Tier 1 Solar Panels , Grid Tie Solar Inverter , AC & DC CABLES

  • Design Type :Structure of Solar Panels Drilling is done at points where the current sheet is bored with columns. Rails support the weight of Solar Panels and are secured with chemical anchoring to prevent water leakage. The Solar Panels are then clamped to the Rails at the joints to make it sturdy.
  • Only Tier I Solar Panels, which use 'A' grade cells with a cell efficiency of over 20%, are integrated and recommended by us. The manufacturers we recommend are global enterprises with gigawatts of installed capacity worldwide, as well as the necessary certifications and a 30-year warranty. We choose solar panels only after we have accessed the manufacturers credibility.
  • The Solar Panel Structure Assembly is drilled at the same time as the current sheet is bored with columns. Rails support the weight of Solar Panels and are secured with chemical anchoring to prevent water leakage.
  • We advise integrating the best German technology inverters because they are the backbone of the solar plant. PV systems that are grid connected are designed to run in parallel with and be connected to the electric utility grid. Inverters of good quality can last up to 25 years.
  • We integrate all of the small and large equipment required for the Solar Power Plant's commissioning. The Panel and Inverter manufacturers follow the installation recommendations and manuals for every equipment, including cabling and installation methodology
