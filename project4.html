82KW on Grid SPV plant Dhoot Arcade , Gurgaon

GENERATION PER DAY : 328 UNITS PER DAY

SAVING PER YEAR : : Rs 838,040/-

TECHNOLOGIES USED : Tier 1 Solar Panels , Grid Tie Solar Inverter , AC & DC CABLES

  • Design Type : Tile arrangement on flat roof tops is the sort of design. Modules are horizontally arranged on the ground and joined in series in the appropriate number of modules. A leg assembly isn't included in the construction frame
  • Only Tier I Solar Panels, which use 'A' grade cells with a cell efficiency of over 20%, are integrated and recommended by us. The manufacturers we recommend are global enterprises with gigawatts of installed capacity worldwide, as well as the necessary certifications and a 30-year warranty. After assessing the manufacturer's credibility, we chose solar panels.
  • The Solar Panel Structure Assembly is drilled at the same time as the current sheet is bored with columns. Rails support the weight of Solar Panels and are secured with chemical anchoring to prevent water leakage.
  • We advise installing the best German technology inverters because they are the core of the solar plant. PV systems that are grid connected are designed to run in parallel with and be connected to the electric utility grid. Inverters of good quality can last up to 25 years.
  • We integrate all of the small and large equipment required for the Solar Power Plant's installation. The Panel and Inverter manufacturers follow the installation instructions and manuals for every equipment, including cabling and installation methodology.
