320KW on distributed SPV plant Guru Harikrishan Public school, delhi

GENERATION PER DAY : 1280 UNITS PER DAY

SAVING PER YEAR : Rs 3,270,400/-

TECHNOLOGIES USED : Tier 1 Solar Panels , Grid Tie Solar Inverter , AC & DC CABLES

  • We only integrate and recommend Tier I solar panels that use 'A' grade cells with a cell efficiency of over 15%. The manufacturers we recommend are global enterprises with gigawatts of installed capacity around the world, as well as the necessary certifications and a 25-year warranty. We select solar panels based on the manufacturer's reputation.
  • We advise integrating the best German technology inverters because they are the backbone of the solar plant. PV systems that are grid connected are designed to run in parallel with and be connected to the electric utility grid. Inverters of good quality can last up to 25 years.
  • We combine all of the small and large equipment required for the Solar Power Plant's commissioning. The Panel and Inverter manufacturers follow the installation recommendations and manuals for every equipment, including cabling and installation methods.
